Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?
SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically begin to appear within 3-6 months. However, some improvements like technical fixes can show results sooner. The timeline depends on factors like website age, competition, and the current state of your SEO.
Do you work with businesses in any industry?
Yes, we have experience working with businesses across various industries including e-commerce, healthcare, finance, technology, and more. Our strategies are tailored to the specific needs and regulations of each industry.
What's included in your monthly reports?
Our monthly reports include key performance indicators, traffic metrics, conversion rates, campaign progress, and recommendations for ongoing improvements. We provide clear, actionable insights to help you understand your ROI.
Do you offer contract flexibility?
Yes, we offer flexible contract terms. Our standard agreement is 6 months, but we can adjust based on your business needs. We believe in delivering value, and our goal is to build long-term partnerships with our clients.
Can I cancel my service at any time?
We require a 30-day notice for cancellation. This allows us to properly wrap up campaigns and provide you with final reports and recommendations. We're committed to ensuring a smooth transition whether you're pausing or ending services.
